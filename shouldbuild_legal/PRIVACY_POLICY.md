# ShouldBuild Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 18 July 2026**

ShouldBuild processes account/profile data, submitted idea and research content, persisted reports and citations, support messages, and technical/provider/error logs needed to operate and secure the service. Paid checkout is unavailable, Dodo Payments is not connected, and ShouldBuild does not currently collect product-transaction or payment-card data.

Depending on configuration and report mode, limited research context and public-source content may be processed by Supabase, Vercel, Google OAuth, Tavily, Firecrawl, Groq, Cerebras, and Cohere. These providers have their own terms and privacy practices. Do not use ShouldBuild as a confidential data room.

Tenant-scoped RLS, private report storage, authenticated access, encryption in transit, restricted server credentials, and operational logging are used as safeguards. No service can guarantee absolute security.

Data-retention periods, deletion SLAs, backup windows, and analytics practices require deployment-specific operational approval and must not be inferred from this draft. Contact **support.shouldbuild@gmail.com** to request access, correction, deletion, or other applicable privacy rights.
