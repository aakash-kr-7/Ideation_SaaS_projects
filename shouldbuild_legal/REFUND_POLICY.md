# ShouldBuild payments and refunds status

**Last updated: 18 July 2026**

Paid checkout, subscriptions, report packs, and one-off purchases are not currently available. Dodo Payments is not connected, so ShouldBuild cannot create a product charge or process a product refund today.

Commercial prices, renewal terms, credit expiry, rollover, taxes, cancellation, and refund terms remain undecided. They must be published and legally reviewed before paid checkout is enabled.

When an entitled research run fails before completion, the database entitlement workflow restores its reserved credit. A negative verdict or disagreement with a report is not a technical failure.

If an unexpected charge appears to reference ShouldBuild, contact **support.shouldbuild@gmail.com** with the descriptor and date. Do not send a complete card number.
